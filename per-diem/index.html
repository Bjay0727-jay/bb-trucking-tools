📝 Per Diem Rules for Truckers
  • Full day rate ($69): Any day you're away from home overnight
  • Partial day rate (75%): Day you leave home and day you return
  • 80% deductible: DOT workers can deduct 80% of per diem (vs 50% for others)
  • No receipts needed: Per diem is a flat rate, no meal receipts required
  • Tax home: Must have a tax home (permanent residence) to claim
